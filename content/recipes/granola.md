- **Servings:** 4 cups
- **Prep:** 15 minutes
- **Cook:** 45 minutes
- **Total:** 1 hour

## Ingredients

- **2 cups** oats
- **3/4 cup** chopped almonds
- **1/8 cup** chia seeds
- **1/3 cup** coconut oil
- **1/3 cup** maple syrup
- **1/4 teaspoon** cinnamon
- **1/4 teaspoon** nutmeg

## Instructions

1. Mix oats, almonds, and chia seeds in a large bowl.
2. In a saucepan, combine the coconut oil, maple syrup, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Mix well.
3. Bring wet mixture to a boil and cut the heat.
4. Pour wet mixture into bowl with dry ingredients and mix well. It should look wet, but not completely soaked.
5. Place mixture on a baking sheet with parchment paper.
6. Bake for 45 minutes at 275° F.
