- **Servings:** 3 breasts
- **Prep:** 1.5 hours
- **Cook:** 30 minutes
- **Total:** 2 hours

## Ingredients

- **1/4 teaspoon** salt
- **1/4 teaspoon** pepper
- **1/4 teaspoon** cumin
- **1/4 teaspoon** garlic poweder
- **1/2 teaspoon** parsley flakes
- **1/2 teaspoon** avocado oil
- **1** lemon
- **3** chicken breasts

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients in a small bowl.
2. Clean chicken breasts thoroughly with vinegar.
3. Place chicken in a ziplock bag and pour in ingredients. Mix well.
4. Let chicken marinate in the refrigerator for about 1 and 1/2 hours. Any more than that and the chicken will start to break apart.

5. Boil water and add a little bit of the same ingredients used on marinade.
6. Place whole chicken breasts (along with marinade) in water until outer part looks cooked.
7. Pull out breasts and cut in half. The insides should still be raw. That is expected.
8. Continue to do this process until you're left with small chunks (to your preferred size) and they are thoroughly cooked. You can check the inner temperature with a thermometer. Cooked temperature should be at least 165° F (74° C). DO NOT let the chicken boil for too long or you risk making it too dry.
