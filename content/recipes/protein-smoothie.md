## Ingredients

- **30g** chia seeds
- **30g** pumpkin seeds
- **15g** cocoa powder
- **5g** cinnamon powder
- **150g** oats
- **50g** walnuts
- **15g** vanilla
- **15g** agave syrup
- **500ml** almond milk
- **240g** frozen berries

## Instructions

1. Blend it all together.
