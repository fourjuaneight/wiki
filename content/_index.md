This **wiki** is a personal knowledge base — a single, reliable place to look up technical information that comes up often enough to warrant a dedicated reference. It is not a tutorial site or a beginner's guide. Each entry assumes familiarity with the subject area and focuses on *how* things work and *why* they are designed the way they are, with citations to primary and authoritative sources wherever claims warrant it.

Coverage spans the topics that appear most frequently in day-to-day technical work: networking fundamentals, security and cryptography, system administration tools, version control, file-sharing protocols, and hardware concepts. Entries are written in prose, formatted for clarity, and kept current. If something belongs here, it should be correct, sourced, and worth returning to.
